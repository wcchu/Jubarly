About

Dr. Wei-Chun Chu is a painter, physicist, data scientist, and musician, now based in Berlin. He was born in Taiwan and has been living and working in the U.S., Ireland, and Germany. He is a senior data scientist of Fit Analytics GmbH, a sizing solution provider for online fashion shops.
